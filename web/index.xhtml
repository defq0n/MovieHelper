What is Movie Helper?

Movie helper is a web based software to help individuals find and pick a movie from a range of choices. Movie Helper allows its users to explore movies that they have never heard of, leave comments, and rate the movies when they are done.

About us

We are a small group of developers at the University of Minnesota Duluth. This project was an idea we came up with to solve a common problem for our software engineering course.
